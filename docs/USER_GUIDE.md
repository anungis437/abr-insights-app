## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit** [abrinsights.ca](https://abrinsights.ca)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top right corner
3. Choose your account type:
   - **Individual Professional** - For personal learning and research
   - **Organization** - For teams and multi-user access
4. Enter your details:
   - Full name
   - Email address (use organization email for team accounts)
   - Secure password
5. Verify your email address
6. Complete your profile setup

### First-Time Setup

After creating your account, you'll be guided through:

1. **Profile Completion** - Add your role, organization, and areas of interest
2. **Platform Tour** - Interactive walkthrough of key features
3. **Initial Assessment** (Optional) - Help us recommend relevant content
4. **Team Setup** (Organization accounts) - Invite team members and assign roles

### Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard provides quick access to:

- **Learning Progress** - Current courses and completion status
- **Recent Activity** - Case searches, course progress, achievements
- **Recommended Content** - AI-powered suggestions based on your role
- **Organization Metrics** (Admin view) - Team progress and analytics
- **Notifications** - Updates, new content, and system messages

## For HR & EDI Professionals

### Exploring Tribunal Cases

**Access the Case Database:**

1. Navigate to **Tribunal Cases** from the main menu
2. Use the search bar to find specific cases:
   - **Keyword search** - Terms like "hiring," "promotion," "harassment"
   - **Advanced filters** - Date range, jurisdiction, outcome, industry
   - **AI classification** - Cases tagged with anti-Black racism indicators

**Understanding Case Details:**

Each case includes:

- **Citation & Source** - Official tribunal reference
- **Parties Involved** - Applicant and respondent (anonymized when applicable)
- **Key Facts** - Summary of the case background
- **Legal Issues** - Grounds of discrimination alleged
- **Decision & Outcome** - Tribunal ruling and remedies ordered
- **AI Analysis** - Automated classification and key themes
- **Related Cases** - Similar decisions for comparison

**Using Cases for Research:**

- **Export Cases** - Download as PDF or CSV for reports
- **Create Collections** - Save relevant cases to custom folders
- **Share Insights** - Generate shareable links for team discussion
- **Track Trends** - Use analytics to identify patterns over time

### Taking Training Courses

**Browsing the Course Catalog:**

1. Go to **Courses** from the main menu
2. Filter by:
   - **Topic** - Anti-Black racism, allyship, legal compliance, etc.
   - **Level** - Foundational, intermediate, advanced
   - **Duration** - Short modules (30 min) to comprehensive courses (8+ hours)
   - **Format** - Self-paced, instructor-led, cohort-based

**Enrolling in a Course:**

1. Click on a course to view details
2. Review:
   - Course outline and learning objectives
   - Duration and time commitment
   - Prerequisites (if any)
   - CE credits available (if applicable)
3. Click **"Enroll Now"** (free courses) or **"Purchase"** (paid courses)
4. Start learning immediately

**Completing Courses:**

- Progress is automatically saved
- Complete modules in any order (unless sequential)
- Take quizzes and assessments to reinforce learning
- Download resources and reference materials
- Earn a certificate upon 100% completion

### Generating Reports

**Compliance & Analytics Reports:**

1. Navigate to **Analytics** > **Reports**
2. Choose report type:
   - **Team Progress Report** - Course completions and engagement
   - **Case Analysis Report** - Trends in tribunal decisions
   - **Compliance Summary** - Training requirements met
   - **Custom Report** - Build your own with specific metrics

3. Configure report parameters:
   - Date range
   - Team members or departments
   - Specific courses or case topics
   - Export format (PDF, Excel, PowerPoint)

4. Generate and download
5. Schedule recurring reports (monthly, quarterly)

## For Trainers & Instructors

### Creating Courses

**Course Authoring Workflow:**

1. Navigate to **Instructor Dashboard** > **Create Course**
2. **Basic Information:**
   - Course title and description
   - Learning objectives (3-5 key outcomes)
   - Target audience and prerequisites
   - Estimated duration

3. **Course Structure:**
   - Add modules and lessons
   - Upload videos, PDFs, and presentations
   - Embed quizzes and assessments
   - Include discussion prompts

4. **Interactive Elements:**
   - Add tribunal case studies
   - Create scenario-based learning
   - Include reflection exercises
   - Embed AI tutor support

5. **Publishing:**
   - Preview course as a student
   - Set pricing (free, one-time, subscription)
   - Publish to catalog or keep private (organization-only)

### Managing Learners

**Tracking Student Progress:**

- View enrollment numbers and completion rates
- See individual student progress
- Review assessment scores and quiz results
- Monitor discussion participation
- Identify struggling learners who need support

**Engaging with Students:**

- Respond to questions in course discussions
- Provide feedback on assignments
- Send announcements and updates
- Host live Q&A sessions (optional)
- Award achievements and recognition

### Using the AI Tutor

The AI tutor is available in all courses to:

- Answer student questions 24/7
- Provide additional explanations and examples
- Suggest related tribunal cases for study
- Generate practice scenarios
- Offer personalized study tips

**Best Practices:**

- Encourage students to try the AI tutor first for quick questions
- Review AI tutor interactions to identify common confusion points
- Update course content based on AI tutor analytics

## For Organization Administrators

### Team Management

**Adding Team Members:**

1. Go to **Organization Settings** > **Team Members**
2. Click **"Invite Members"**
3. Enter email addresses (bulk upload via CSV supported)
4. Assign roles:
   - **Admin** - Full access to organization settings
   - **Manager** - Can view team analytics and reports
   - **Instructor** - Can create and manage courses
   - **Member** - Access to enrolled courses and case database

5. Set permissions and course access
6. Send invitations

**Managing Subscriptions:**

- View current plan and usage
- Add or remove seats
- Upgrade/downgrade plan
- Manage billing information
- View invoice history

### Organization Branding

**Customize Your Portal:**

1. Navigate to **Organization Settings** > **Branding**
2. Upload your organization logo
3. Choose brand colors (primary and secondary)
4. Customize welcome message
5. Add custom domain (Enterprise plan)

### Analytics Dashboard

- **Compliance Status** - Training requirements met, certifications earned
- **ROI Analysis** - Time invested, knowledge gained, behavior change
- **Trend Analysis** - Progress over time, departmental comparisons

**Custom Dashboards:**

- Create role-specific views (HR, Leadership, EDI Committee)
- Schedule automated reports
- Export data for presentations
- Set goals and track progress

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is ABR Insights only for Canadian organizations?**  
A: While our tribunal case database focuses on Canadian human rights law, the training courses and best practices are valuable for organizations globally combating anti-Black racism.

**Q: Do I need technical expertise to use the platform?**  
A: No! ABR Insights is designed for all users. If you can use email and browse websites, you can use our platform.

**Q: Can I try ABR Insights before purchasing?**  
A: Yes! We offer a free tier with access to limited courses and case database features. Organization demos are available upon request.

### Account & Billing

**Q: Can I switch between individual and organization accounts?**  
A: Yes, contact our support team to migrate your individual account into an organization account.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), PayPal, and invoicing for organizations (annual plans only).

**Q: Do you offer non-profit or educational discounts?**  
A: Yes! Contact us at [sales@abrinsights.ca](mailto:sales@abrinsights.ca) for custom pricing.

### Courses & Content

**Q: Are courses self-paced or scheduled?**  
A: Most courses are self-paced, but we also offer cohort-based courses with scheduled start dates.

**Q: Do you offer CE credits or professional certifications?**  
A: Selected courses offer continuing education credits. All courses provide certificates of completion.

**Q: Can I suggest new course topics?**  
A: Absolutely! We welcome suggestions at [content@abrinsights.ca](mailto:content@abrinsights.ca).

### Tribunal Cases

**Q: How often is the case database updated?**  
A: We update the database weekly with new tribunal decisions.

**Q: Can I access full tribunal decision texts?**  
A: Yes, full decisions are available with direct links to official sources.

**Q: Are cases AI-classified reviewed by experts?**  
A: Yes, our AI classifications are validated by legal experts and continuously improved through human feedback.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**  
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions).

**Q: Is my data secure?**  
A: Yes! We use enterprise-grade security, encryption, and are compliant with Canadian privacy laws (PIPEDA).

**Q: Can I export my data?**  
A: Yes, you can export your learning progress, certificates, and saved cases anytime.
